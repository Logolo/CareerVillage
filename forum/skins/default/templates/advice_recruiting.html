What is CareerVillage?

CareerVillage is an online Q&A platform that allows busy professionals, like you, to easily answer questions about their career and help hundred of students.

What we recognized at CareerVillage was that many students were graduating without feeling prepared for any career. We work with schools to help their students get ready for careers, and we need your help.

When you sign up today you’ll be joining a community of over 500 professionals dedicated to this cause, breaking the cycle of poverty for low-income students.
