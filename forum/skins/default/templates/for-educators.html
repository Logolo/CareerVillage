For Educators: Get CareerVillage in your Classrooms!

Provide your students with access to a wide array of professionals for FREE.
 

What is CareerVillage?

CareerVillage is an online community where students ask professional development questions and working professionals answer them. It is supported by a dedicated non-profit. Our mission is to help students explore longer-term career options and the tangible steps they can take to make those options a reality.

How does the program work?

We provide our partners with all of the materials they need to run a 6-week CareerVillage program with their students. It with a workshop on asking good questions, and then is followed up with easily-or-automatically-gradable homework assignments or friendly competitions to encourage students to get used to the experience of asking questions.
