Our founding team: The Village Chiefs

 

Jared Chung - Chief Village Elder

Jared is a Village Founder and Chief Village Elder at CareerVillage. He is an entrepreneur, self-taught developer, and advisor to non-profits. He holds a B.S. in Finance from NYU Stern, where he first began working with education organizations on a pro-bono basis.

Jared was previously an Engagement Manager with McKinsey & Company, where he managed teams on projects in a wide range of industries across a dozen countries over a 6 year period. While at McKinsey, he built cross-functional joint ventures, led training seminars, and led pro-bono consulting projects.

In his spare time, Jared organizes TEDx conferences, rides his bike in the beautiful rolling hills around the village, and surveys his lands as Chief Village Elder.


Jennifer Pan - Chief of Village Ops

Jennifer is a Village Founder and Chief of Village Operations at CareerVillage. She is a graduate student in the Department of Government at Harvard University. She holds a B.A. summa cum laude from the Woodrow Wilson School of Public Policy and International Affairs at Princeton University.

Jennifer's research interests include welfare state development in non-OECD countries and the politics of non-democratic states, with methodological interest in automated content analysis and experiments. Prior to her graduate studies, she was an engagement manager at McKinsey & Company based in New York and Beijing. Jennifer has also worked for the Clinton Foundation HIV/AIDS Initiative and Chinese Center for Disease control.

When Jennifer is off-duty, she maintains the Village's stable of wild gyphons.

Our founding board members: The Village Council

 

Jessica Pliska is the Founder and Executive Director of the Opportunity Network and sits on the organization's board of directors. Before founding The Opportunity Network in 2002 with Brian Weinstein, Jessica worked with non-profit organizations that include The "I Have a Dream" Foundation, Prep for Prep, God's Love We Deliver, The New York City Ballet and a variety of community organizations focused on economic development in inner city neighborhoods. Jessica graduated from Yale University with a B.A. in political science. She sits on the board of One to World, a non-profit organization creating learning and cultural opportunities for Fulbright and other international scholars in New York City, and on the Education Advisory Board of the "I Have a Dream" Foundation.

 

Michael Park is a Principal at McKinsey & Company, the global management consulting firm. He is a leader in McKinsey's Corporate Finance and Advanced Industrials practices, advising energy equipment providers, contractors, utilities, and governments on growth strategy, cleantech, M&A and divestitures. He also leads the Firm's work on US innovation and growth for the Advanced Manufacturing sector. Mr. Park graduated magna cum laude from Harvard College with an A.B. in Economics.
