Founding board members

 

Jessica Pliska is the Founder and Executive Director of the Opportunity Network and sits on the organization's board of directors. Before founding The Opportunity Network in 2002 with Brian Weinstein, Jessica worked with non-profit organizations that include The "I Have a Dream" Foundation, Prep for Prep, God's Love We Deliver, The New York City Ballet and a variety of community organizations focused on economic development in inner city neighborhoods. Jessica graduated from Yale University with a B.A. in political science. She sits on the board of One to World, a non-profit organization creating learning and cultural opportunities for Fulbright and other international scholars in New York City, and on the Education Advisory Board of the "I Have a Dream" Foundation.

 

Michael Park is a Principal at McKinsey & Company, the global management consulting firm. He is a leader in McKinsey's Corporate Finance and Advanced Industrials practices, advising energy equipment providers, contractors, utilities, and governments on growth strategy, cleantech, M&A and divestitures. He also leads the Firm's work on US innovation and growth for the Advanced Manufacturing sector. Mr. Park graduated magna cum laude from Harvard College with an A.B. in Economics.
